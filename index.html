I'm an Assistant Professor at the Department of Management Science at Antai College of Economics and Management, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I got my Ph.D. degree in Supply Chain, Operations and Technology from Olin Business School, Washington University in St.Louis in 2022. Before that, I received my B.S. in statistics from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2016.

My research focuses on revenue management problems in data-rich business settings with applications in asset selling platforms (e.g., used cars, real estate) and retailing platforms, and information issues in risk management (including the contexts of supply chain finance, blockchain implementation, etc.).
